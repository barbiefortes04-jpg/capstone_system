# Code of Conduct

We expect all contributors and participants to follow these basic guidelines to foster a welcoming, inclusive, and professional community.

Expected behavior
- Be respectful and courteous in discussions, issues, and pull requests.
- Be collaborative and constructive when giving or receiving feedback.
- Credit others for their ideas and work.

Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language will not be tolerated.
- Sharing private information without consent, or exposing credentials or secrets.

Enforcement
- Report violations by opening an issue or contacting project maintainers. Maintain a factual, timestamped record where possible.
- Maintainers may remove or block users who repeatedly violate this policy.

Contact

This project values diversity and inclusion. Thank you for helping keep the community welcoming for everyone.
